Payment FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our payment process

How long does it take to receive my purchase?

After your payment is confirmed by our admin team, your purchase will be activated within 15 minutes during active hours (9 AM - 11 PM GMT+7). Outside of these hours, it may take up to 24 hours.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we don't store any of your payment information. All transactions are handled directly through the respective payment platforms (PayPal, DANA, etc.) or through our admins who follow strict security protocols.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds within 24 hours of purchase if the product has not been used. Please contact our admin team via Discord with your purchase details to request a refund.

What payment methods are available for international players?

International players can use PayPal for all purchases. Some regions may also support other payment methods - contact our admin team on Discord to discuss alternative options if needed.

How do I know my payment was received?

Our admin team will confirm your payment receipt on Discord. You'll also receive the purchased items on the server, and can check with /rank or appropriate commands to verify your purchase.
